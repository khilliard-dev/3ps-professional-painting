Meet Myles

Myles is the owner of 3P's Professional Painting, serving homes and businesses across the Carolinas'. With years of hands-on experience in interior and exterior painting, drywall repair, and pressure washing, Myles and his team brings precision, reliability, and satisfaction to every job.

What sets Myles apart is his commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. Whether it’s a full home repaint or a small touch-up, he treats every project with the same level of professionalism and pride.

Service areas include: Manning, Sumter, Columbia, Orangeburg, Camden, and surrounding areas.
